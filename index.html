Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 2026 · v1.0.0
📋 Overview
Spectrum Slither collects absolutely no personal data.
No name. No email. No location. No device ID. No cookies. Nothing.

This policy explains how Spectrum Slither handles your information when you play the game. We believe in complete transparency — so this document covers everything.

🎮 What data we collect

None. Spectrum Slither does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you.

Your score and high score are stored only in your device's local memory during the current session. They are never sent to any server and are wiped when you close the app.

🤖 AI Commentary (Claude API)

Spectrum Slither features live commentary powered by PRISM, an AI commentator built on Anthropic's Claude API.

When PRISM generates commentary, a short game event description is sent to our secure backend server, which then calls Anthropic's API. Examples of what is sent:

  • "Game started"
  • "Player scored 10 points"
  • "Snake died at score 25"
This text contains no personal information — only game state data. Your name, device details, and location are never included.

Anthropic's own Privacy Policy governs their handling of API requests on their servers.

🔒 Security

All communication between the game and our backend server is encrypted via HTTPS. Our backend server:

  • Never logs your IP address or device information
  • Never stores any game data or session history
  • Only accepts requests from the Spectrum Slither app
  • Rate limits requests to prevent abuse (max 20 commentary calls per day)
👶 Children's Privacy

Spectrum Slither is rated Everyone (E) and is appropriate for all ages. Since we collect no personal data whatsoever, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy.

We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you are under 13, you can play safely — nothing about you is collected or stored.

📢 Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used by Spectrum Slither is the Anthropic Claude API for AI commentary. We do not use:

  • Advertising networks or ad SDKs
  • Analytics or tracking services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Social media SDKs (Facebook, Twitter, etc.)
  • Crash reporting services
  • Any form of user tracking or fingerprinting
🔄 Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be updated and the app version number will be incremented.

Continued use of Spectrum Slither after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

📬 Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? You can reach the developer via the contact information listed on the Google Play Store page for Spectrum Slither.

We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 48 hours.
